About Me

Hello! I'm Nethmini Kaushalya the creative eye behind Texas Digital. I'm a passionate photographer with a deep love for capturing real, raw, and meaningful moments that turn into timeless stories. With over two years of hands-on experience, I’ve worked on a variety of projects ranging from weddings and portraits to commercial and scenic photography.

Currently, I’m pursuing a BSc in Multimedia at NSBM Green University, where I continue to sharpen my technical skills and explore the limitless possibilities of visual storytelling. My academic background has enriched my understanding of design, cinematography, and digital media—skills that I blend seamlessly into my work behind the camera.

I've had the privilege of working as a photographer for several companies, where I gained valuable industry experience and collaborated with clients to bring their visions to life. My style combines artistic expression with attention to detail, aiming to create images that not only look beautiful but feel authentic.

At Texas Digital, I don’t just take pictures—I craft visual experiences that resonate. Whether you're looking to document a special event, capture your unique personality, or tell a brand story through imagery, I’m here to make it happen with passion and precision.

Let’s connect and turn your moments into memories, one frame at a time.
